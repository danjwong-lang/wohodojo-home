Why I built this

The sensei origin story

After 25 years of trading, I was still juggling a dozen different tools just to answer one simple question: should I buy this stock or not?

I was venting about this to Kerry, a longtime friend and fellow investor. We'd both been frustrated with the same thing for years. The good tools cost a fortune. The free ones were either too complicated or too shallow.

"There should be one tool that looks at everything that matters and just tells you: buy, hold, or get out of the way."

So I built it. WoHoDojo is the tool we wanted to exist. It's free because good information shouldn't cost you $100 a month before you've made a single trade.

We use it for our own trades every week. We hope you find it useful too.

— Daniel-San, founder of WoHoDojo
